ALBERO is the only tree restaurant in Kazakhstan. The restaurant is located in the Katon-Karagai State National Nature Park near the Rakhmanovskiye Kluchi sanatorium. Visiting Albero, you can enjoy both passive and active recreation, appreciate the gorgeous mountain-forest landscape and the futuristic architecture of the building. The menu of ALBERO is dominated by Italian cuisine but also includes multicultural dishes. We are waiting for guests both from all over Kazakhstan and abroad.
